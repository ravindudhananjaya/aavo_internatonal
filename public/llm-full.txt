## Mission
To bridge the gap between authentic Asian producers and the Japanese market, providing high-quality, halal-certified meats and essential groceries to restaurants and retailers.

## Website Structure & Routes

### Main Pages
- **Home (`/`)**: Introduction, Featured Products, Call to Actions.
- **About (`/about`)**: Company story, mission, and corporate profile.
- **Categories (`/categories`)**: Visual grid of product categories (Meat, Rice, Spices, etc.).
- **All Products (`/products`)**: Comprehensive list of all available products.
- **Catalog (`/catalog`)**: Gate for downloading the full wholesale price list (requires simple form).
- **Contact (`/contact`)**: Contact form and direct contact details (WhatsApp, Viber).

### Legal Pages
- **Privacy Policy (`/privacy`)**: Data collection and usage.
- **Terms of Service (`/terms`)**: Usage agreement for wholesale accounts.
- **Refund Policy (`/refund`)**: Returns only for defective/incorrect items (perishable goods).
